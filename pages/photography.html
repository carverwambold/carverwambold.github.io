My Journey in Photography

I started taking photography more seriously as a hobby during and after my trip to Alaska, for which I bought my main backpacking camera, a Sony-RX100VII. This camera went through it on the trip, but is still in great shape. Since then, I've been handed down a Cannon 70D, with the 70-200mm f/4-5.6 kit lens, and a Nikon N75 with a Tameron 28-200mm f/3.8-6.3 lens. Recently I've been using a combination of these three cameras to take photos wherever I go or whatever I do.

Gear I Use

  • Camera: Canon EOS 70D
  • Lens: Canon EF 70-300mm f/4-5.6 IS USM
  • Film Camera: Nikon N75
  • Lens: Tamron 28-300mm f/3.5-6.3
  • Point and Shoot Camera: Sony Cyber-Shot RX100 VII
  • Editing Software: Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop
